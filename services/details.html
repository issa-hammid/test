Planning & Scheduling

TAKT Time Schedules

Transform Traditional Scheduling into Flow-Oriented Execution

ATM develops TAKT-based time schedules that transform traditional scheduling into highly efficient, location-based, flow-oriented execution plans. Our approach creates predictability, reduces waste, and ensures seamless coordination across all trades.

TAKT planning is a revolutionary approach to construction scheduling that borrows principles from manufacturing to create predictable, efficient workflows. Unlike traditional scheduling methods that focus on individual activities, TAKT planning organizes work by location and creates a rhythm that all trades follow.

Our Process

A systematic approach to implementing TAKT planning in your projects

01

Project Analysis

We analyze your project scope, constraints, and requirements to understand the full context and identify opportunities for TAKT implementation.

02

Zone Definition

Break down the project into logical zones of similar size and complexity to create a balanced workflow foundation.

03

TAKT Time Calculation

Calculate optimal TAKT time based on project deadlines, available resources, and zone characteristics.

04

Work Balancing

Balance workload across all trades to ensure each can complete their work within the TAKT time.

05

Schedule Development

Create detailed visual schedules showing the flow of work through zones over time.

06

Implementation Support

Provide training and ongoing support to ensure successful adoption and execution of the TAKT plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to implement TAKT planning?

Initial planning typically takes 2–4 weeks, depending on project complexity. Full implementation and team training usually requires 4–6 weeks.

Is TAKT suitable for all types of construction projects?

TAKT works best for projects with repetitive elements like multi-story buildings, residential complexes, or infrastructure with similar sections. We assess each project to determine suitability.

What tools do you use for TAKT planning?

We use a combination of specialized TAKT software, visual planning tools, and digital collaboration platforms tailored to your project needs.

How do you handle changes during execution?

TAKT plans are flexible and can accommodate changes. We establish protocols for managing variations while maintaining overall schedule integrity.
